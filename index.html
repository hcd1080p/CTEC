Overview

CLSU Testing and Evaluation Center

The CTEC is envisioned to serve as a centralized venue for psycho-education testing, performance evaluation, and academic research in support of the academic program. It also provides appropriate testing services for screening incoming students and personnel and provides psychological assessment for special cases among students, faculty, and staff needing psychological testing.
